Places to go for summer of 2025

  • Arcade Main Event

    • Main event is an arcade that has a variety of games for you and your family to play on. Not only that, it has some great food included as well.

  • Six Flags Hurricane Harbor Splashtown

    • This would be perfect for the summer weather. There are a bunch of water slides and pools for people in all ages.

  • Sun Valley, Idaho

    • The weather over in idaho is pleasently warm, it is recommended though that you do spent time there on June and not July since its the hottest month.
